Who We Are

Built by Someone Who Gets It

Austin Humphrey grew up in Memphis, moved to Texas, and spent years watching coaches struggle with outdated tools and scattered data. As a University of Texas graduate who loves the Cardinals, Titans, Longhorns, and Grizzlies, he knew there had to be a better way.

So he built Blaze Sports Intel—the platform he always wished existed. No BS. No fluff. Just the intelligence you need to prepare your team, evaluate talent, and make the right call when it matters most. From Boerne, Texas to programs across the Deep South, Blaze is helping teams win more games.
